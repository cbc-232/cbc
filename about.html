Company overview

From Local Impact to Sustainable Innovation Across Africa and the Globe.

Founded by Sulaiman Ndamisa in 2023, CyberBiz Clinic Ltd. began as a community-driven initiative at Koidu City Library and has evolved into Sierra Leone's leading multi-unit tech ecosystem. Our mission is to bridge the gap between technology and real-world needs, delivering accessible, intelligent, and scalable solutions that transform communities across Africa and the World at scale.

Our approach pairs local insight with international standards: From Sierra Leonean needs to the African region and globally.

Our structure

We operate across six specialised units that collaborate to deliver both high-impact services and future-ready products:

  • EmpHub: Digital literacy & Professional Skills
  • SaniTech: IT Consultation, Support, Custome Web & App
  • DigCizen: Digital identity, Website Management, & Optimization.
  • Security & Intelligence Agency (S.I.A): Cybersecurity & Intelligence.
  • AccessEcom: E-commerce platforms & Payment gateways
  • Sierra Agentic Agency (S.A.A): AI Agents, Automations & Workflow
