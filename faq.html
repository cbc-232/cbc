General

Clear, straightforward answers about CyberBiz Clinic Ltd. — who we are, how we work, and what drives us forward.

What is the core mission of CyberBiz Clinic Ltd.?

We exist to harness technology and innovation for positive social impact - building solutions that empower communities in health, education, governance, and digital transformation across Africa and beyond.

How is CyberBiz Clinic Ltd. structured to deliver on its mission?

Our structure balances visionary leadership with agile execution:
Board of Directors guides strategy and governance.
C-Level Executives steer company-wide operations and innovation.
Unit Heads lead specialized teams across technology and service lines.
Specialists execute focused projects with deep expertise.
Interns & Ambassadors fuel creativity, research, and community outreach.

What values define the culture at CyberBiz Clinic Ltd.?

We operate on a foundation of integrity, innovation, and social responsibility. Our team embraces resilience, transparency, and a growth mindset. We are committed to learning from challenges and delivering meaningful impact.

How do different units collaborate within the company?

Collaboration is at our core. Our units work together across different functions, from AI development to digital literacy programs. Teams share insights, integrate solutions, and align their efforts to maximize impact for our clients and communities. This cross-functional approach ensures that every department contributes to our shared mission of positive social change.

How can someone become part of CyberBiz Clinic Ltd.?

We welcome passionate individuals through internships, ambassador programs, and specialist roles. Keep an eye on our team's page and announcements, or submit an open application emphasizing your skills and vision for impact.

What is the company’s approach to growth and innovation?

We believe in calculated risks, continuous learning, and embracing emerging technologies to build scalable solutions. Our focus is on sustainable growth that benefits our clients, communities, and the broader ecosystem.

Services

Clear answers about the range of services CyberBiz Clinic Ltd. offers — how they work, who they benefit, and why they matter.

What services does CyberBiz Clinic Ltd. provide under IT consultation and support?

We offer comprehensive IT consultation and support services including infrastructure planning, technology audits, digital transformation roadmaps, and cybersecurity readiness assessments. Our technical support ensures your systems remain stable, secure, and scalable as your business grows.

Do you develop both web and mobile applications?

Yes. Our custom development services include creating responsive websites, full-stack web platforms, and mobile apps for Android and iOS. We also integrate features like user dashboards, admin panels, and APIs for seamless operations.

What does your Digital Literacy training cover?

Our Digital Literacy training programs teach practical skills in essential digital tools, online safety, cloud-based collaboration, and modern communication platforms. We customize training for individuals, educational institutions, and community groups to meet their specific needs and bridge the digital divide effectively.

What’s included in Website Management and Optimization?

This service ensures your site runs efficiently and performs well in search rankings. It includes speed optimization, regular content updates, security maintenance, UX/UI improvements, and SEO enhancements.

Do you offer cybersecurity services for SMEs?

Yes. We offer cybersecurity solutions that are specifically designed for small and medium-sized enterprises. We provide comprehensive protection that includes penetration testing, real- time threat monitoring, secure data handling, and employee awareness training. All our cybersecurity services are tailored to meet your industry's specific security requirements and compliance needs.

What are AI Agent Solutions and how do they work?

AI Agent Solutions are intelligent automation tools we custom-build to handle repetitive tasks such as customer service queries, data entry, and workflow management. They improve efficiency, accuracy, and response time.

Products

Clear insights into CyberBiz Clinic Ltd.’s product offerings — what they do, who they serve, and the impact they create.

What is CyberBiz Clinic Ltd. really about?

CyberBiz Clinic Ltd. exists to turn technology into a tool for inclusive progress. We design digital products that solve real problems in healthcare, waste management, and learning—making access possible for everyone, not just the privileged few.

Who is LifeSync for?

LifeSync is for patients, hospitals, and medical personnel. It shortens wait times by letting people book appointments, call ambulances, and make inquiries—often just using a regular phone. Doctors and hospitals can also monitor patient records and follow up remotely. It’s smart healthcare, made simple and accessible.

Can I use your platforms without a smartphone?

Yes—LifeSync and CleanUp are designed to work even on basic phones via USSD, SMS, and IVR (voice calls). However, V-Hub is a full-featured learning platform and requires access via a web browser or mobile app. It cannot be accessed via SMS or voice.

Are your products available now or still in development?

All three platforms—LifeSync, CleanUp, and V-Hub—are currently in advanced development. Public rollouts are coming soon, and you can join the waitlists to be part of early testing and feedback phases.

How are these products different from existing solutions?

Unlike off-the-shelf platforms, our solutions are context-aware and designed for Sierra Leone’s realities. We support both smart and basic phone users, integrate with local systems, and put accessibility first—without compromising innovation.

How can I get involved or stay in the loop?

You can join our beta community, sign up for product updates, or partner with us if you’re a hospital, school, waste agency, or entrepreneur. We’re always looking for people and organizations who believe in bold, practical change.
