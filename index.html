About CyberBiz Clinic Ltd.

Empowering progress through technology

Founded in Koidu, CyberBiz Clinic Ltd. is a multi-unit technology company dedicated to solving real-world challenges with practical, scalable digital systems. We design, develop, and deliver Websites & Mobile Applications, AI Solutions, Digital Literacy Programs, E-commerce Platforms, Cybersecurity Services, and Digital Automation Tools for governments, NGOs, startups, and enterprises across Sierra Leone, West Africa, and beyond.

Our approach combines local insight with international standards, delivering technology that fits local contexts and performs globally.

We are committed to:

  • Building systems designed to scale and adapt.
  • Training people to thrive in digital spaces.
  • Partnering with institutions to modernize operations and improve outcomes.

Every solution we provide is grounded in integrity, driven by purpose, and designed for impact.

FAQs

What problems does CyberBiz Clinic Ltd. solve?

We solve practical problems across healthcare, waste management, learning, and public service delivery. We deliver tailor-made services (web, cybersecurity, AI, training) and build products—LifeSync, CleanUp, and V-Hub—that address recurring local challenges.

How can I request work or get a quote?

Send a brief to info.cbc.sl@gmail.com or use our contact form stating your needs and timeline. Our intake team will follow up with scope options and next steps.

Can I join early pilots for your products?

Yes. We run pilot programs for partners and early adopters. Visit the product pages to join waiting lists and pilot invitations.

Do your solutions work in low-resource settings?

We build for constraints: offline-first designs, SMS/USSD fallbacks, small-footprint apps, and low-bandwidth UX. Pilots validate performance before scaling.

How do I stay updated on launches and events?

Subscribe to updates on the site, follow our social channels, or join product mailing lists for launch news, events, and partnership opportunities.
