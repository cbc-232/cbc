F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer under the Free Plan?

Our Free Plan includes basic digital literacy training and assistance with creating a single-page website. It’s designed for individuals or small businesses just starting out online.

What is included in the Starter Plan?

The Starter Plan offers advanced digital literacy training, including website development with up to 5 pages, and access to basic digital marketing tools to get your business online.

Can I upgrade my plan at any time?

Yes, you can easily upgrade your plan at any time to access more features and services. We offer seamless transitions between our pricing tiers.

What is the difference between the Business and Ultimate Plans?

The Business Plan includes all services from the Starter Plan, plus a custom website with up to 10 pages, intermediate SEO support, and monthly analytics reports. The Ultimate Plan offers all-inclusive services, including full-scale e-commerce setup, advanced digital marketing, and priority customer support.

Do you offer custom solutions for businesses?

Yes, we offer custom solutions for businesses that require specialized services. Contact us directly to discuss your specific needs and we’ll tailor a plan that works best for you.

How can I subscribe to your newsletter?

You can subscribe to our newsletter by filling out the form on our website. We send regular updates about our services, new offerings, and industry insights.

Is there any support available for free users?

Yes, we offer limited email support for users on the Free Plan. However, paid plans come with dedicated customer support for faster response times and more comprehensive assistance.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings. Once canceled, your plan will remain active until the end of the current billing cycle.
