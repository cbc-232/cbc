FAQs

Clear answers about the range of services CyberBiz Clinic Ltd. offers — how they work, who they benefit, and why they matter.

What kind of IT consultation and support do you offer?

Our IT consultation covers infrastructure planning, system audits, and long-term digital strategies. We also provide ongoing technical support for organizations seeking stability, scalability, and data security.

Can you build both web and mobile apps for my organization?

Yes. Through our Custom System Build service, we develop full-stack web platforms and mobile applications tailored to your needs, including admin panels, user portals, and API integrations.

How does your Digital Literacy training work?

We host workshops and bootcamps that teach practical skills in digital tools, online safety, and modern communication platforms. Programs are designed for beginners, students, and community organizations.

What’s included in your Website Management and Optimization service?

This service includes performance monitoring, content updates, security patches, SEO enhancements, and UX improvements — ensuring your site runs smoothly and ranks well across search engines.

Do you provide cybersecurity services for small and medium enterprises?

Absolutely. We offer scalable cybersecurity solutions including penetration testing, threat monitoring, and data protection protocols tailored to the size and nature of your business.

What are AI Agent Solutions and how can they help my business?

Our AI Agent Solutions are custom-built tools that automate tasks like customer support, data entry, and operational workflows. They reduce manual load and improve consistency and response time.
